A. P . J Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Missile Man of India

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam ( English : APJ Abdul Kalam ), also known as the Missile Man and the People's President , was the eleventh elected President of the Republic of India. [3] He was known as the former President of India , a well-known scientist and engineer . He taught that no matter what the situation may be in life, but when you are determined to fulfill your dreams, you keep on fulfilling them. The thoughts of Abdul Kalam Masoodi still inspire the young generation to move forward. He primarily handled the Defense Research and Development Organization (DRDO) and the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) for four decades as a scientist and science administrator, and was also involved in India's civilian space program and military missile development efforts. . He is known as the 'Missile Man' of India for his work in the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology. [4] He played a decisive, organizational, technical and political role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear test in 1998 for the second time since the first original nuclear test by India in 1974. [5] Kalam was elected President of India in 2002 with the support of both the ruling Bharatiya Janata Party and the opposition Indian National Congress . [6] After serving a five-year term, he returned to his civilian life of teaching, writing and public service. He received many prestigious awards including the Bharat Ratna , India's highest civilian award.
